FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the Leo Club?

Richmond College students may apply for membership through our club advisor or directly contact any executive committee member. We welcome enthusiastic individuals committed to service and leadership.

Are there membership fees?

Yes, a nominal annual membership fee applies to cover Lions International affiliation, Leo district dues, and club administrative costs. Details are provided at the time of application.

Can I volunteer even if I am not a member?

Absolutely! We welcome community volunteers for specific projects and events. Please contact us with your interest and we will get back to you with upcoming volunteer opportunities.

How can my organisation sponsor a project?

We appreciate corporate and individual sponsorships. Please reach out via our contact form selecting "Sponsorship" as the inquiry type, and our team will share our sponsorship packages with you.
