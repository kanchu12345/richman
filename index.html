About Us

Leading The Way
For A Better Tomorrow

The Centennial Leo Club of Richmond College is a vibrant youth-led organization committed to leadership, volunteering, and service. Through impactful projects, community initiatives, and teamwork, we strive to uplift lives, empower youth, and build a better tomorrow as part of the global Leo and Lions movement.
